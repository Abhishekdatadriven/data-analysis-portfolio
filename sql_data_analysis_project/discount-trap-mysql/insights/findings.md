4. Discount Trap Products

Products classified as Discount Traps met the following criteria:

*Revenue Growth > 0

These products represent high commercial risk despite growing sales.


5. Business Interpretation
Revenue growth driven by discounting is not sustainable.
While discounts increase short-term demand, they erode long-term profitability and distort true product performance.


6. Recommendations

*Review discount strategies for identified discount trap products

*Introduce margin-based KPIs alongside revenue metrics

*Test price elasticity before applying blanket discounts

*Prioritize products with healthy growth and stable margins
